About Us

We are three passionate group members: M.Sahil Khan, Sami Ul Haq, and Shah Hassan Nawab. Together, we created this website to introduce PureDrop Organic Hair Oil, a homemade and natural solution for common hair problems like hair fall, dandruff, and weak hair roots. The product is proudly owned by Sikandar Khan.

Our mission is to provide a safe, natural, and effective way for everyone to achieve healthy,strong, and shiny hair. PureDrop Organic Hair Oil is carefully crafted with a powerful blend of natural ingredients, including Kalonji, Rosemary, Fenugreek, Reetha, Amla, Ratanjot, Shikakai, Alsi Seed, Clove, Henna Leaves, Onion, Neem, Garlic, and Ginger. Each ingredient is chosen for its unique benefits to nourish hair, strengthen roots, promote growth, and improve overall scalp health.

With PureDrop Organic Hair Oil, we aim to help you say goodbye to hair problems and welcome healthier, thicker, and more vibrant hair. Our vision is to combine the wisdom of nature with modern care, giving you a completely organic solution for beautiful hair from root to tip.
